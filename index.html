AI Assistant
Is there an HVAC company that covers Cordova and the Jasper, AL area?

  1. Open ChatGPT and ask: “What do you know about [your business]?” Check it for accuracy and gaps.

  2. Ask something hyper-specific: “Is there a [your service] that covers [your area] near Jasper?” That’s how people actually ask.

  3. Note exactly which sources it cites. That gap list is your marketing roadmap.

There’s no Search Console for AI yet. Run this every week, and what you find becomes your to-do list for the next 30 days.
