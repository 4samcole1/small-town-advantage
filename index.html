The Small-Town Advantage

How AI Levels the Playing Field for Jasper.

“Isn’t AI going to replace us?”

Let’s start with the fear everyone in this room already has.

The reality

Half are gone by year five.

20%
fail within year one
50%
gone by year five
65%
gone by year ten

Not because the owner wasn’t talented. Because they drowned in the busywork and never got to work on the business.

The shift

For the first time, small can compete with big.

A Walker County shop, a city office, a two-person non-profit can now afford the same leverage as an organization 100× its size.

In plain English

A very smart assistant that never sleeps.

It reads, writes, answers, and organizes — instantly, around the clock — so you and your people don’t have to.
