12AEO gets you found in AIThe rules just changed
The single most “forward-thinking” moment of the talk. Slow down here.
Say — the shift
  • “For twenty years, the game was SEO — you optimize your site with keywords so it shows up when someone Googles ‘roofer in Jasper.’ They get a list of blue links, and they pick one.”
  • “But your customers aren’t only Googling anymore. They’re asking an AI — ChatGPT, Google’s AI Overviews, Perplexity, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot — ‘Who’s the best roofer near Jasper, Alabama?’”
  • “And the AI doesn’t hand back ten links. It gives one answer, and it names a business. The whole game becomes: are you the answer? That’s AEO — Answer Engine Optimization.”
Say — the key idea: fluff vs. fact (this is on the slide)
  • “Here’s what matters: AI filters out generic claims. It synthesizes specific data, real outcomes, and proof. Same business, two ways to describe it —”
The fluff: “We’re the best local plumber. Premium service, high quality.” — invisible to AI.

The fact: “We replaced 40 water heaters last month. Average response: 45 minutes. 92% customer satisfaction.” — this is what gets cited.
The fluff: “Expert, personalized accounting services you can trust.”

The fact: “We filed 300 small-business returns this year. Zero IRS penalties. Clients saved an average of $4,200 in missed deductions.”
  • “Only one version gives the AI real numbers to repeat. Prove it with real numbers — and FAQs that answer the exact questions your buyers ask. That’s the whole game.”
Why it works (your credibility — say if asked / if the room’s technical)
  • AI answers weight tangible, structured signals: detailed & recent reviews, quantified claims, certifications, warranties, transparent pricing, response times, consistent business info across the web, third-party mentions, and machine-readable structured data (schema).
  • Our AEO work is a real audit: we score a site on exactly those signals — review depth & recency, schema coverage, claim specificity, entity consistency, third-party citations — and engineer them up, so the business becomes the one the AI recommends. (fine-tune this line to a real audit metric you use)
  • Bottom line to say: “SEO gets you on the list. AEO makes you the answer — and most businesses have done zero about it. That’s the open door right now.”
Transition: “Do all five of these together, and here’s what happens…”
